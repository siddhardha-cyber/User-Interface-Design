Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is a premier top-ranked private multi-disciplinary deemed university in India, founded by Sri Mata Amritanandamayi Devi (AMMA). It is accredited 'A++' by NAAC and offers over 250 programs in engineering, medicine, sciences, and humanities across 10 campuses. Known for research, it ranks high in QS World University Rankings.
