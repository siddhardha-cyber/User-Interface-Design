Amrita Vishwa vidyapeetham, Coimbatore

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore (established 1994) is a top-ranked, NAAC A++ accredited private multi-disciplinary deemed university in Ettimadai, Tamil Nadu. It is known for its research-intensive focus, 400-acre scenic campus, and strong engineering programs, often ranking among India's top 10 universities in the NIRF rankings.
