Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Amritapuri

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Amritapuri Campus, located in Vallikavu, Kerala, is a premier, NAAC A++ accredited, 80-acre research-intensive institution founded by Mata Amritanandamayi Devi. It offers diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs, specializing heavily in engineering, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity, with a strong focus on societal impact and technology.
