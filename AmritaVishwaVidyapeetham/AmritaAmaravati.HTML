Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Amaravati

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Amaravati is a premier, NAAC A++ accredited, research-intensive institution located near Mangalagiri, Guntur district, Andhra Pradesh, focusing on engineering, technology, and sciences. Part of a vast multi-campus system, it offers cutting-edge programs like BTech (CSE/AI/Data Science), BCA, MSc in Data Science/Physics, and doctoral studies with a strong emphasis on technology and societal research.
